# Napier Art Deco - New Zealand
<Gallery album=2 sliceStart=0 sliceEnd=1 />
Napier, New Zealand is renowned as the Art Deco Capital of the World. This distinction stems from a devastating earthquake in 1931 that leveled the city.  In the aftermath Napier was rebuilt in the Art Deco style resulting in a remarkably cohesive and striking collection of architecture.   

<Gallery album=2 sliceStart=1 sliceEnd=3 />

Walking through Napier feels like stepping back in time. 

Napier's Art Deco isn't a strict imitation of European trends. It incorporates Maori motifs and design elements inspired by the work of architect Louis Hay, an admirer of Frank Lloyd Wright, creating a distinctive "Napier Style."   

<Gallery album=2 sliceStart=8 sliceEnd=20 />

The heritage is a testament to the city's ability to overcome adversity and rebuild with optimism and style. This spirit is palpable in the vibrant atmosphere and the pride locals take in their unique architectural legacy.

<Gallery album=2 sliceStart=3 sliceEnd=8 />

If you're interested in architecture, design, or simply want to experience a place unlike any other, Napier is a must-visit destination.
